Legend of Maula Jatt:>

Lollywood Goes International with This Blockbuster.


The story is about Maula, a wrestler who wants revenge for his parents. He teams up with his friend Mooda to take on the bad guys. One notable character in the film is Noori, the older brother of the antagonists. Noori is portrayed as a formidable figure, hardened by years of self-imposed isolation in prison. Despite his tough exterior, he holds a deep affection for his sister Daaro and is fiercely protective of her. The movie is full of action from start to finish. Maula and the villains fight in epic battles that keep you on the edge of your seat.

"The Legend of Maula Jatt" stands as a testament to the power of Pakistani cinema, captivating audiences with its breathtaking action sequences and compelling characters.
